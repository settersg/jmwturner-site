**A painting speaks from the auction block.**

It is 1997. In a small auction house in western Canada, a huge, dark, foreboding canvas is put up for sale. Nobody wants it. But one man sees something in the storm-tossed survivors clinging to life. He buys it for next to nothing.

What follows is a five-year odyssey through the shadowy corridors of the art world. The painting may be a lost masterpiece by J.M.W. Turner. Or it may be worthless. The answer depends on who you ask.

The journey leads from auto-wrecking yards to Christie's. From forensic fingerprint labs to the Tate Gallery. From Interpol undercover stings to a weeklong confrontation with the world's foremost Turner expert.

**Who decides what is real?**
