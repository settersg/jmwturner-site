 ** Everyone associated with the 
 Art World has their own story to tell. Regularly, we will publish the most 
 thought-provoking and informative of those sent in, while giving full credit 
 to the author.

 topics extending beyond issues pertaining specifically to JMW Turner.**

 house where J.M.W. Turner lived and worked." It would be the modest 
 structure with the large view windows. On his final day, the sun beamed in
