## The Story in 36 Chapters

*Art World's Dirty Little Secret* unfolds across eight narrative arcs — from the painting's dramatic discovery at auction to the weeklong confrontation with Christie's senior consultant. Each chapter is a taste of the book. Read them in order, or dive into any arc that calls to you.

**[Part 1: The Discovery →](/chapters/the-discovery/)** — A painting speaks from the auction block. Frosty's auto-wrecking yard. Tate Gallery rejection. Media controversy.

**[Part 3: Science & Proof →](/chapters/science-proof/)** — Forensic fingerprints. Hidden monograms. When science meets connoisseurship.

**[Part 4: The Provenance Trail →](/chapters/provenance-trail/)** — Christie's archives. James Orrock's collection. A chain of ownership back to Turner.

**[Part 6: Christie's →](/chapters/christies/)** — The auction house that controls the market. Gatekeepers close the gates.

**[Part 7: The Butlin Visit →](/chapters/butlin-visit/)** — A week with Christie's senior consultant. Verbatim dialogue. The dramatic climax.

**[Part 8: Portrait & Aftermath →](/chapters/aftermath/)** — Turner's self-portrait hidden in the rescue boat. Art world tribal instincts. The final reckoning.
