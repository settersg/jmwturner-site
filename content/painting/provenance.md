**                        
Christie's Stenciled Stock Number found on the stretcher of *
[Shipwreck, the 
Rescue](Intro%20Shipwreck%20JMW%20Turner%20painting.htm)***

 

                            ** From 
Christie's Archivist,
[Jeremy Rex-Parkes**](http://www.justterriers.com/volume1number3_stencils.html), 
16 Mar 2001:

**"Good 
Morning Mr. Setters; It was 73OAS and the sale was 4th June 1904**, 
**The Highly Important Collection of Pictures and Watercolour Drawings**,
**the property of** **
[James Orrock](James%20Orrock%20Sir%20J.C.%20Robinson%20connoisseurship%20J.M.W.%20Turner%20Shipwreck%20Paintings.htm)**, **owing to ill health and is ordered away from London, 
Lot 140, JMW Turner, RA, *The Shipwreck*   63 X 87 inches.**

**
   Presented by the artist to his executor, Mr. Griffith.**

**
   From the collection of Sir J. C. Robinson.**

**   I hope the information is what you were looking for, **

**
   and if you have other stock numbers, **

**
   do not hesitate to contact me. Regards jrp."    **

      

**
