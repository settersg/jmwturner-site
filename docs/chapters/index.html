The Story in 36 Chapters

Art World’s Dirty Little Secret unfolds across eight narrative arcs — from the painting’s dramatic discovery at auction to the weeklong confrontation with Christie’s senior consultant. Each chapter is a taste of the book. Read them in order, or dive into any arc that calls to you.

Part 1: The Discovery → — A painting speaks from the auction block. Frosty’s auto-wrecking yard. Tate Gallery rejection. Media controversy.

Part 2: The Criminal Past → — A fugitive owner. Undercover stings. Interpol and the Mounties.

Part 3: Science & Proof → — Forensic fingerprints. Hidden monograms. When science meets connoisseurship.

Part 4: The Provenance Trail → — Christie’s archives. James Orrock’s collection. A chain of ownership back to Turner.

Part 5: Expert Politics → — The Unknown Turner. British art establishment. When experts guard their turf.

Part 6: Christie’s → — The auction house that controls the market. Gatekeepers close the gates.

Part 7: The Butlin Visit → — A week with Christie’s senior consultant. Verbatim dialogue. The dramatic climax.

Part 8: Portrait & Aftermath → — Turner’s self-portrait hidden in the rescue boat. Art world tribal instincts. The final reckoning.
